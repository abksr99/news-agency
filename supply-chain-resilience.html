Customer Impact & Communication

Supply chain adjustments may affect customer experiences in several ways:

  • Delivery Times: Temporary extensions during transition periods
  • Product Availability: Some items may have limited stock
  • Pricing Adjustments: Slight increases to cover diversification costs
  • Enhanced Tracking: Improved visibility into shipment status
üéØ Customer Service Focus: Emphasize improved long-term reliability and reduced risk of major disruptions. Customers value supply chain stability over short-term cost savings.

Agent Guidance

When discussing supply chain topics with customers:

  • Frame changes as proactive improvements, not reactive measures
  • Highlight enhanced reliability and reduced future disruption risks
  • Provide specific timelines for any temporary impacts
  • Offer alternative solutions when primary options are affected
